About Us




Duroguard line of steel doors is an establishment of Ecoline Infra private limited as a private entity with a clear focus on bringing modernization and sustainability to the millions of Indian homes. The founders’ excitement for a global outlook but maintaining the cultural roots is evident in bringing the established international standards to the taste of the Indian consumers.

We take immense pride in introducing ourselves as pioneer manufacturers of steel doors in India focused on bringing the best of the world’s engineering advancements to Indian homes. Ecoline is renowned for supplying a wide range of Duroguard premium quality steel doors at very competitive prices in the market. Each of our products has the strength of steel and the beauty of the wood offers you long-lasting quality and ultimate security to your home.

Right from the inception, we strive to establish sustained innovation, customer-oriented, and commitment to deliver top-notch products in terms of price, quality, durability, and security. Our premium range of steel doors has been regarded as a quality parameter and trendsetter for the whole industry.
